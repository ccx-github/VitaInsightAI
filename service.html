OUR PRODUCT -- Beatwise

Our system focuses on multi-factor cardiovascular risk prediction rather than simple rhythm screening, enabling a more holistic assessment of heart health. By applying interpretable AI, the model not only identifies elevated risk but also explains why the risk increases and provides actionable guidance for early self-intervention. This approach is supported by comprehensive data integration, unifying demographic information, detailed self-reported symptoms, and high-resolution physiological signals into a single, coherent cardiovascular risk-assessment stream.

  • PREDICTIVE -- beyond screening.
  • INTERPRETABLE -- to the decision level.
  • INTEGRATED -- across signals.

Analysis of A Sample Report

User does not have cardiovascular risk. Shapley Value Waterfall Charts help explain what factors contribute to the risk of CVD. Start by looking at the baseline score, which shows the average risk without any specific data. Each row represents a factor, like blood pressure or cholesterol, that affects risk. Red bars mean the risk goes up, blue bars mean it goes down, and the length of the bar shows how much it impacts the risk. The final score combines all factors to give the overall CVD risk.You can use these charts to spot your personal risk factors. Look for the longest bars—these are the most important. Compare the factors on the chart, like age or cholesterol, with your own health data. If you find several factors that raise your risk, consider talking to a doctor. Keep an eye on these factors over time to catch potential problems early.
